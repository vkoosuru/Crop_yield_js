The Website

Yield Prediction

On the website, the power of machine learning to predict crop yield based on several crucial factors is utilized. By inputting the district name, soil nutrients, irrigation level, temperature, rainfall, and crop name, our advanced ML algorithm generates accurate yield predictions. District name helps us consider the specific geographical location and its impact on crop performance. Soil nutrients play a vital role in determining the fertility and suitability of the soil for a particular crop. The irrigation level allows us to assess the water availability and its influence on plant growth. Temperature and rainfall are significant climate indicators that directly affect crop development. Lastly, the crop name provides essential information about the specific plant species under consideration. By leveraging these inputs, our ML model analyzes historical data, patterns, and relationships to make insightful predictions on crop yield. This enables farmers and agricultural stakeholders to make informed decisions and optimize their agricultural practices for improved productivity.

The Data




In the absence of an existing dataset for Telangana state,
significant efforts were undertaken to compile a comprehensive dataset for the region.
Data collection was done from various official resources.The collected data
was then meticulously organized, analyzed, and transformed into a dataset specifically
tailored for Telangana. This dataset is now provided on our website,
offering valuable insights and enabling users to access reliable information for informed decision-making
in the field of agriculture.
