The Data


In the context of data collection for crop yield prediction, the state of Telangana presented a unique challenge. Despite the wealth of available data for various regions, there was a significant data gap specifically for Telangana. Recognizing the importance of accurate predictions for this region, extensive efforts were undertaken to collect and compile data solely for Telangana. The aim was to gather comprehensive information on crucial parameters such as temperature, rainfall, irrigation levels, and district-specific characteristics. By bridging this data gap, this dataset was made to ensure that Telangana farmers could benefit from reliable crop yield predictions tailored specifically to their region.
